Privacy Policy: The Intimacy Standard

1. Data Minimization

We only require email addresses and chosen names. We do not track location data or store credit card numbers on our local servers; all payments are handled via encrypted third-party gateways (Stripe/PayPal).

2. "Active Status" Visibility

By default, your Verification URL shows "ACTIVE" or "DISSOLVED." You can toggle "Private Mode" in your Dashboard to hide your names from the public search, showing only your Bond ID to anyone scanning your QR code.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Solomon Kin LLC never sells intimacy data. Your partnership status is a private asset. We do not share bond records with advertisers, social media platforms (other than your chosen Kin Pages display), or data brokers.
