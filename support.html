Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my Bond to Kin Pages?

In your Vault, click "Export for Kin Pages." This generates a high-res badge with your unique QR code that fits perfectly in a social media profile slot.

What if my partner refuses to sign?

A Bond cannot be sealed without mutual consent. If the second party does not sign within 72 hours, the draft is purged and the initiation fee (if applicable) is held as credit in your account.

Is this a legal marriage?

No. As per our Legal Disclaimer, this is a private record of commitment. It provides social and digital standing but does not change your legal marital status with the government.
